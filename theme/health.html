  • Cholesterol or Triglyceride Lowering Medications
    Why is this important?

    Do not use Crestor OTC along with any of these medicines. Taking Crestor OTC could cause an interaction with these drugs which could lead to side effects.

    What does this mean?

    Crestor OTC is a statin and should not be used with other statins such as atorvastatin, fluvastatin, lovastatin, pitavastatin, prvastatin, rosuvastintin and simvatatin.

  • Cyclosporine
    Why is this important?

    Do not use Crestor OTC along with cyclosporine. Taking cyclosporine with Crestor OTC may significantly increase the amount of the active ingredient of Crestor OTC in your blood. This could lead to side effects..

    What does this mean?

    Cyclosporine is a medicine primarily used in people who have had organ transplants.

  • Warfarin
    Why is this important?

    Do not use Crestor OTC along with warfarin. Taking Crestor OTC with warfarin may affect the way warfarin works. This could lead to side effects.

    What does this mean?

    Warfarin, sometimes called COUMADIN®, is a medicine that helps treat and prevent blood clots.
